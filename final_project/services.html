Our Services

Explore our variety of pottery services designed for everyone

Pottery Teaching Services

At Ember Ceramic, we offer a variety of teaching services designed to introduce you to the magical world of pottery or help you refine your existing skills. Our experienced instructors provide personalized guidance in a supportive environment.

Wheel Throwing Classes

Learn the fundamentals of wheel throwing with our expert instructors. Our classes are designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced potters looking to refine their techniques.

Each session includes:

  • Personalized instruction on centering, pulling, and shaping clay
  • Guidance on trimming and finishing techniques
  • Access to professional-grade wheels and tools
  • Clay and firing included in course fees

Classes are available in 6-week sessions or as intensive weekend workshops.

Hand Building Workshops

Explore the versatile techniques of hand building in our specialized workshops. Perfect for those who prefer working without a wheel or want to complement their wheel throwing skills.

Our hand building workshops cover:

  • Pinch pot, coil, and slab building methods
  • Surface decoration and texture techniques
  • Sculptural approaches to clay
  • Functional pottery creation without a wheel

These workshops are perfect for all ages and abilities, making them ideal for family activities or team-building events.

Glazing and Finishing Techniques

Turn your pottery pieces into colorful works of art with our specialized glazing workshops. Learn how different glazes interact and how to achieve unique effects through various application methods.

These sessions include:

  • Introduction to different types of glazes
  • Application techniques including dipping, pouring, and brushing
  • Understanding glaze interactions and layering
  • Special effects like wax resist and oxide staining

Students can bring their own bisque-fired pieces or use studio-provided items to practice on.

Private Lessons

For those seeking personalized instruction or wanting to focus on specific techniques, we offer one-on-one lessons tailored to your individual needs and goals.

Benefits of private instruction include:

  • Customized learning plan based on your interests
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Accelerated learning through focused attention
  • Perfect for working through challenges or preparing for exhibitions

Private lessons can be booked as single sessions or as packages for ongoing development.

Studio Access and Equipment Rental

For experienced potters who need access to equipment, we offer studio time rentals that include the use of wheels, tools, and kiln firing services. Monthly memberships are available for regular users, providing discounted rates and priority scheduling.

Our Teaching Philosophy

At Ember Ceramic, we believe in nurturing creativity while teaching solid technical foundations. Our approach combines structured instruction with plenty of room for individual expression and exploration. We create a supportive community where students can learn from each other as well as from our instructors.
