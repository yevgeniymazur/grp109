Welcome to Ember Ceramic

A vibrant community pottery studio in the heart of Seattle

What We Offer

Classes & Workshops

From beginner to advanced, our classes cater to all skill levels. Learn wheel-throwing, hand-building, and glazing techniques.

Studio Memberships

Enjoy 24/7 access to our facilities, equipment, and glazes with our flexible membership options.

Private Events

Host your next team building event, birthday party, or special occasion in our inspiring creative space.

Custom Orders

Commission our talented artists to create bespoke ceramic pieces for your home or business.

A Place to Make Art

What better place to make clay art than an old brick building nestled in a park full of old growth trees, beaches and wildlife? Ember Ceramic has been a cornerstone of the local arts community since 1986, incorporated as a non-profit educational ceramics institution and run by a dedicated Board of directors.

Our studio provides a serene and inspiring environment where creativity can flourish. Join our community of artists and discover the joy of working with clay.
