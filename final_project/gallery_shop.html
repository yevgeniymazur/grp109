Gallery & Shop

Welcome to Ember Ceramic's Gallery & Shop, where craftsmanship meets creativity. Explore our collection of unique, handmade pottery pieces—each one carefully crafted to bring warmth and beauty into your home. Whether you're looking for functional pottery or one-of-a-kind artistic creations, our gallery showcases a variety of items available for purchase. Take your time to browse, and find the perfect piece to add to your collection or gift to someone special.

Gallery

Welcome to the Ember Ceramic Pottery Gallery, open every Friday and Saturday from 4 PM to 10 PM. Our gallery proudly displays a variety of handcrafted pottery pieces created by both students and local artists who use our studio. Twice a year, we host a major sale event on December 10th and August 20th, featuring over 50 pottery artisans showcasing their work. Join us for these special events to meet the artists, see their creations up close, and enjoy a one-of-a-kind shopping experience.
