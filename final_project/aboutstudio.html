Our Studio

Based in Seattle, Ember Ceramic is owned and operated by a group of artists. They offer a variety of clay classes, including hand-building and wheel-throwing techniques, and host various events. Offer classes, memberships, workshops, and events. We also provide options for private classes and corporate group events. We offer 24/7 access to the facilities for members.

A Place to make art

What better place to make clay art than an old brick building nestled in a park full of old growth trees, beaches and wildlife? Seward Park Clay Studio has been incorporated as a non-profit educational ceramics institution and run by a Board of directors since 1986.

Our Philosophy

At Ember Ceramic, we believe that working with clay is not just about creating beautiful objects—it's about connecting with an ancient craft that grounds us in the present moment. Our approach to teaching emphasizes mindfulness, technical skill, and creative exploration.

We welcome artists at every stage of their journey, from complete beginners to professional ceramicists. Our instructors are practicing artists who bring their unique perspectives and specialties to the classroom, ensuring a rich and diverse learning experience.
