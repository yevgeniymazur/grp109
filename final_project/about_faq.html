FAQ

If you have any questions, please review our FAQ section below.
Please don't hesitate to call us at 425-555-9898, you can leave a message, and we will return you within 24 hours.

What do the 6-week pottery classes include?

Our 6-week pottery classes are designed for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced artists. Each class meets once a week for two hours, where you will learn pottery techniques, including handbuilding and wheel-throwing. You’ll have the opportunity to create your own pieces, with guidance from our experienced instructors, and all materials and firing costs are included in the class fee.

Can I host a corporate event or team-building activity at your studio?

Yes! We offer corporate morale events and team-building parties at our pottery studio. These events are customized to foster creativity and collaboration in a fun, relaxed setting. Whether you want a one-time pottery session or a more involved creative challenge, we can tailor the experience to suit your team’s needs.

Do you host birthday parties?

Absolutely! We host fun and memorable pottery birthday parties for all ages. Whether you want a creative hands-on experience or simply a fun, relaxing atmosphere, we will customize the party to make it special. You can choose to create pottery together or just enjoy a relaxing time in our studio with your friends and family.

Can I buy pottery pieces from your studio?

Yes, we have a variety of handmade pottery pieces available for purchase in our studio. From functional mugs and bowls to decorative sculptures, each piece is created by local artists. Our inventory is updated regularly, so be sure to stop by to see what’s new!

Do you accept custom pottery orders?

Yes, we offer custom pottery orders! Whether you're looking for a unique gift, personalized home décor, or custom pieces for your business, we can work with you to bring your vision to life. Just contact us with your idea, and we’ll discuss the details, including size, design, and timeline.

Can I purchase pottery items online?

Yes, our online store offers a selection of pottery items that you can purchase from the comfort of your home. You can browse our collection, order directly through the website, and have your pottery pieces shipped to you. Keep an eye on our site for new items being added regularly!
